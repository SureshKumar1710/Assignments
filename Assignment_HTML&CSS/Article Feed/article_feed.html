Movies To Watch

Discover some of the most iconic and unforgettable movies of all time!

Ironman (2008) – 7.9/10

Genius inventor Tony Stark builds a high-tech suit to become the armored superhero Ironman, battling villains and his own demons. A thrilling start to the Marvel Cinematic Universe!

Titanic (1997) – 7.9/10

A timeless love story unfolds aboard the doomed Titanic as Jack and Rose fight for love and survival against the odds.

Scarface (1983) – 8.3/10

Tony Montana rises to power in Miami’s drug world, only to face the consequences of his unrelenting ambition. A gritty tale of greed and downfall.

The Matrix (1999) – 8.7/10

A hacker discovers the shocking truth about reality and joins a rebellion to free humanity from a simulated world.

Avatar (2009) – 7.9/10

Journey to Pandora, where a marine-turned-avatar fights to protect an alien world and its people from destruction.

Deadpool (2016) – 8.0/10

The merc with a mouth delivers hilarity and chaos as he takes on enemies with irreverent humor and an arsenal of weapons.

Red Notice (2021) – 6.3/10

An art thief, an FBI agent, and a con artist form an uneasy alliance in a globetrotting hunt for priceless treasures.

Jaws (1975) – 8.0/10

A great white shark terrorizes a small beach town, and a group of men must work together to stop the deadly creature. Spielberg’s iconic thriller that set the standard for suspenseful horror.

Aladdin (2019) – 6.9/10

A street-smart young man discovers a magical lamp and teams up with the wisecracking Genie, played by Will Smith, to win the heart of Princess Jasmine. A live-action adaptation of the Disney classic, full of adventure and magic.

Johnny English (2003) – 6.2/10

A bumbling British spy, Johnny English, takes on an impossible mission to save the world with his ridiculous antics and unintentional heroism. A hilarious parody of spy thrillers.

Dunkirk (2017) – 7.8/10

Christopher Nolan’s epic war drama depicting the harrowing evacuation of Allied soldiers from Dunkirk during World War II. A visually stunning and intense narrative.

Lucy (2014) – 6.4/10

Scarlett Johansson stars as Lucy, a woman who gains superhuman abilities after a drug alters her brain capacity. A gripping sci-fi thriller that explores human potential.
